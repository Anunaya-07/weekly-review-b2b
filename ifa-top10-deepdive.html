Feature Overview · NCD IPO Subscriptions
What is NCD IPO?
Non-Convertible Debenture IPOs allow distributors to subscribe to primary debt issuances from corporates directly through the platform. Distributors can place subscription orders on behalf of their clients during the open IPO window.
